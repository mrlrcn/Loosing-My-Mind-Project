About the project

I decided to create a project where the secondary part of the picture becomes the main part.

Who

Ibizan artist Giuseppe Pepe has launched a campaign called Loosing My Mind, a series of picture perfect photo manipulations in which he has taken the heavy editing process to its logical conclusion: removing the face entirely. He calls the eerie effect of faceless bodies in Instagram-optimizied scenarios as 'anti-beauty.'

What and why

In an era where everything is fake, where everything is designed to appear, even a simple photo has lost its spirit due to the editing.
